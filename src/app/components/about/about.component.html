Our mission is to provide an Online Processing System for the Barangay Pulong Sta. Cruz

Barangay Pulong Sta. Cruz currently processes paperwork in the traditional manner due to the absence of a system in place which results in consuming a significant amount of a resident's day or week in processing their statutory documents.

By providing a designed and developed system that allows online processing of statutory
documents, it will help the personnel to process documents needed in a minimum amount of time.

Our Background

The Team are the aspiring students of the Asia Technological School of Science and Arts under College of Engineering and Information Technology Education Department. The website will be the fulfillment of our final output on our Capstone project under supervision of our Department OIC Rozaida C. Tuazon and the Professors under the CEITE Department.

Our Core

Our Team have seen the potential to provide an online processing of documents in the Barangay Pulong Sta. Cruz, Laguna. While many of us has a limited time to process on a face-to-face manner. The project will be a profound processing system in the Barangay to enhance the technological skills and to innovate the traditional manual processing of documents.
